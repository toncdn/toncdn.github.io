How it works

  1. Create TON wallet
  2. Choose content
  3. Approve traffic costs
  4. Consume content and share it with others to receive TON's
  5. Payments will be made with an accuracy up to KB* using payment channels
  6. Withdraw your earned TONs on-chain or pay off-chain
